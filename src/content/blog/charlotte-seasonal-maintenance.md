<h2>Charlotte-Specific Challenges</h2>

<h3>Pollen (Spring)</h3>
<p>
    Charlotte's heavy pollen season clogs gutters, coats everything yellow,
    and affects HVAC systems. Clean frequently!
</p>

<h3>Humidity (Summer)</h3>
<p>
    High humidity promotes mold. Use dehumidifiers in basements/crawl
    spaces. Monitor closely.
</p>

<h3>Freeze/Thaw Cycles (Winter)</h3>
<p>
    Unpredictable temperature swings damage pipes, gutters, and concrete.
    Extra vigilance needed.
</p>
